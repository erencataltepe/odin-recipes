Karnıyarık

Description

Karnıyarık (lit. 'riven belly' in Turkish) is a dish found in Turkish cuisine[1] consisting of eggplant stuffed with a mix of sautéed chopped onions, garlic, black pepper, tomatoes, optional green pepper, parsley and ground meat. The dish is often served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 4 medium eggplants
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
