Kayseri Manti

Description

Mantı is a traditional Turkish dumpling dish, especially famous in Kayseri. It consists of small dough pockets filled with seasoned ground meat, boiled, and then served with a garlicky yogurt sauce and butter infused with red pepper flakes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water (approx.)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 200 g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 small onion, finely grated
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes (pul biber)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, egg, salt, and water. Knead until a smooth dough forms. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix the ground meat, grated onion, salt, and pepper to prepare the filling.
  3. Roll out the dough into a thin sheet. Cut small squares (about 2–3 cm).
  4. Place a tiny amount of filling in the center of each square. Pinch the edges together to form small dumplings.
  5. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the mantı for 8–10 minutes, until they float.
  6. Mix yogurt with minced garlic for the sauce.
  7. In a small pan, melt butter and stir in red pepper flakes.
  8. Drain mantı, place on a plate, top with yogurt sauce, and drizzle with butter-pepper mixture.
